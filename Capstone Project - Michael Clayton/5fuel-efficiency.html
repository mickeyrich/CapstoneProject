Fuel Efficiency

Toyota Camry

Experience the Camry!

Experience the perfect blend of fuel efficiency, reliability, and performance with the Toyota Camry—a sedan that redefines the joy of driving.

The Toyota Camry is a remarkable sedan in terms of fuel efficiency. With an EPA-estimated 51 mpg in the city, 53 mpg on the highway, and a combined rating of 52 mpg, it strikes an impressive balance between performance and eco-friendliness. Powered by a 2.5-liter inline-four engine and dual electric motors, it delivers a combined output of 208 hp and 163 lb-ft of torque. Whether you’re commuting or embarking on a road trip, the Camry Hybrid ensures fewer stops at the pump and a greener driving experience.

Lexus NX 450h+

Indulge the NX 450h+!

Indulge in luxury without compromise and embrace eco-conscious sophistication with the Lexus NX 450h+—where efficiency meets elegance in every drive.

The Lexus NX 450h+ is a remarkable hybrid SUV that seamlessly blends luxury and efficiency. With an impressive 84 MPGe (miles per gallon equivalent) and a combined fuel economy of 36 mpg, it caters to eco-conscious drivers without compromising on performance. The advanced hybrid powertrain combines a 2.5-liter engine with electric motors, delivering a total system output of 302 horsepower. Whether you’re navigating city streets or embarking on a road trip, the NX 450h+ ensures a smooth and fuel-efficient ride, all wrapped in Lexus’s signature elegance.

Toyota Tacoma

Tacoma Off-Road Fun!

Experience rugged reliability and fuel efficiency combined in the Toyota Tacoma, your trusted companion for urban adventures and off-road escapades alike.

The Toyota Tacoma is a reliable midsize pickup truck that strikes a balance between rugged capability and fuel efficiency. With an EPA-estimated 18 mpg in the city and 22 mpg on the highway, it caters to both urban commuters and off-road enthusiasts. Whether you’re navigating city streets or tackling rough terrains, the Tacoma ensures a dependable ride while keeping fuel consumption in check.
